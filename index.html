/ WHO I AM

Designing with science at the core

I'm a Product Designer with a Cognitive Science background from UC San Diego, now pursuing my graduate studies at USC's Integrated Media Arts program. My academic foundation in how humans perceive, think, and decide directly shapes every design decision I make.

我是一名产品设计师，本科就读于加州大学圣地亚哥分校认知科学专业，目前在南加大IYA项目攻读研究生学位。认知科学的学术背景让我从人类感知与决策的角度出发，深刻影响着每一个设计决策。

What sets me apart is the rare combination of UX research depth, visual design craft, and marketing intuition. I've led end-to-end product design across consumer apps, while simultaneously running multi-platform marketing campaigns in both North American and Chinese markets.

我的独特之处在于将深度用户研究、视觉设计能力与市场营销直觉融为一体。我曾主导多个消费类应用的端到端产品设计，同时在中美两个市场运营多平台营销活动。

I'm fluent in both English and Mandarin, skateboard in my downtime, and believe the best products are built at the intersection of empathy and data.
